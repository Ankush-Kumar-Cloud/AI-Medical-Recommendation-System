About Health Care Center

Who We Are

Health Care Center is an AI-powered platform designed to help users understand their health in a smarter way. By analyzing symptoms, our system provides possible disease predictions and helpful guidance.

What We Offer

  • Precaution recommendations
  • Medication suggestions
  • Workout guidance
  • Personalized diet plans
